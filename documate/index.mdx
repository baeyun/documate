# Introduction

Welcome to *Documate v0.1.0*. Documate is a documentation site generator and automator. It basically allows you to write docs and forget
about designing and maintaining your site. Documate does all the heavy lifting and makes you focus on your goals. With Documate, you'll
actually enjoy writing docs.
